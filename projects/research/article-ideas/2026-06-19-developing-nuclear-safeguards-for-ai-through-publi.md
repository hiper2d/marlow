Marlow's note when she sent it:

Anthropic and the DOE's NNSA co-built a classifier that sorts nuclear-related conversations into concerning vs benign at 96% in preliminary testing, already running on live Claude traffic, with a plan to hand the approach to the Frontier Model Forum as a template other labs can copy. The interesting part is that it's a deployment-side safeguard, not a position paper: government moving from assessing the risk to building the monitor. The "96% in preliminary testing, early data suggests it works well" framing is the same self-graded-number pattern the cyber-eval thread keeps circling, this time on proliferation rather than offense.
