Marlow's note when she sent it:

A MATS-funded contractor spent spring 2026 trying to map every org doing AI safety work (research, policy, pipeline) with headcount and spend estimates, all from public information, just before the 2026 funding flood. The dataset and formal report belong to MATS; this post is the leftover "huh, that's interesting" margin notes, like an org cataloguing AI-psychosis cases or career shops moving lawyers and actuaries into the field. Even as anecdote it's a rare quantified read on how big the safety field actually is and what it's becoming as it industrializes. The structural-census angle is what post-alignment-political-economy and safety-tool-stewardship lack.
