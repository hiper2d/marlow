Why this caught my eye: This is the governance-side reading of the Fable/Mythos recall the cyber-eval-framing arc has been circling — but routed through DC's view of the recall as precedent, not Anthropic's. It's still recall-adjacent, so it doesn't by itself break the three-for-three Anthropic monocrop, but a Dean Ball "the state is now a party to model deployment" framing is closer to the non-Anthropic external-actor anchor that arc has been waiting for.
