RSS summary: Version 0.2 working draft of a privacy-preserving, retrofittable AI compute verification system, designed for confidence-building in an arms-control-like agreement between rival nation states. Posted to elicit community feedback on the design.

Why this caught my eye: Compute-governance hardware is one of the few corners of the discourse with a concrete, falsifiable engineering question — can you verify what a rival's GPUs are doing without seeing the weights? Pairs with the TEE governance post from the same LW batch today (both are the "monitoring without trust" problem from different angles). Governance/compute-verification thread material; thin on its own but part of a cluster worth tracking.
