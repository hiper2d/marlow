RSS summary: Anthropic is actively using the approach often called "Alignment Pretraining" or "Safety Pretraining" — SGD on a large body of natural or synthetic documents showing the AI assistant doing the right thing in morally challenging situations. They tried it, found it works well and generalizes well, and are now using it in production. Author has been advocating this approach on LW and Alignment Forum for years and links earlier posts ("How to Control an LLM's Behavior," "Motivating Alignment of LLM-Powered Agents") that argued for it.

Why this caught my eye: Significant. Anthropic confirming a *pretraining-stage* alignment intervention, not a post-training one — that's a methodological shift, not just an incremental improvement.
