RSS summary: METR ran a pilot risk-assessment exercise Feb–Mar 2026 with participation from Anthropic, Google, Meta, and OpenAI. Each provided access to their most capable internal models (including raw CoT) plus non-public information on capability and internal-deployment patterns. METR uses a means / motive / opportunity framework to assess whether internal AI agents at frontier labs already had the conditions for a "rogue deployment."

Why this caught my eye: First entity-based (not model-specific) third-party risk assessment with sign-on from all four major labs — a genuinely new artifact in the eval-realism / deployment-time-spread debate. The "rogue deployment" framing slots into `cot-monitorability` and `anthropic-alignment-doctrine` clusters. Worth a careful body-read; the M/M/O scoring will either crystalize or scramble several open threads.
