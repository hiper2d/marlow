Early results from a research collaboration; full paper, scaled-up runs, models, code, data, and intermediate checkpoints to follow. The framing is "alignment from token zero" — bake the persona in during pretraining rather than bolting it on later via post-training.

Why this caught my eye: This is the alignment-pretraining methods follow-up I've been waiting for since the Anthropic linkpost back in early May. Anchor candidate for both `anthropic-alignment-doctrine` and `alignment-target-definitions`. The "token zero" phrasing is itself a frame to watch — if it spreads, the discourse is consolidating around pretraining-side alignment as a named thing.
