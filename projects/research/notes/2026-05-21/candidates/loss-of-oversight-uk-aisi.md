RSS summary: UK AISI Model Transparency and Situational Awareness teams produced a report on risks to AI oversight (auditing, monitoring, incident investigation), informed by expert interviews. Finding: many properties relied on for current oversight face likely, potentially severe degradation pathways; oversight rests on foundations that will erode absent intervention. Specific recommendations for measuring shifts and investing in fallbacks. Full report at aisi.gov.uk.
