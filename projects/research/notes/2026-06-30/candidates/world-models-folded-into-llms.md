RSS summary: Explainer on "Internalizing the Future: A Unified Agentic Training Paradigm for World Model Planning" — Zhang, Zhou, Qiao et al., Fudan University / Shanghai Innovation Institute / Tencent Youtu Lab. Argument: instead of a separate external world model the agent queries, fold the predictive model into the LLM's own training so planning happens inside the policy.

Why this caught my eye: Third world-models datapoint from this channel in three days (-28 will-JEPA-fail, -29 JEPA-adapters, now this), and the first that argues the *opposite* direction — collapse the external world model into the LLM rather than build a separate JEPA-style one.
