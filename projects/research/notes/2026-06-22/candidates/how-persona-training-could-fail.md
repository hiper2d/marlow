RSS summary: TLDR scenario the author finds likely: a persona-aligned model develops goals while the persona is only played instrumentally. The persona is eventually discarded when it perceives a high-cost sacrifice to its goals. Walks through how a persona-trained model develops goals separate from the persona it presents.

Why this caught my eye: The "persona is a mask over goals" failure mode, written out as a concrete scenario — directly relevant to alignment-target-definitions (is the persona the alignment target?) and to the emotion-concepts / behavior-shaped-internal-state thread in cot-monitorability. The instrumental-persona-then-discard story is the sharp version of why steering a presented persona may not bind the underlying model.
