RSS summary: Anthropic launches Claude Tag, a Slack-embedded agent (beta for Enterprise/Team, on Opus 4.8) that joins channels as a shared "@Claude" teammate — multiplayer, persistent channel memory, ambient/proactive behavior, and asynchronous task execution (it can schedule tasks for itself and "pursue a project autonomously over hours or days"). Replaces the existing Claude-in-Slack app. Notable: "65% of our product team's code is created by our internal version of Claude Tag," and the team now "delegates tasks to many Claudes in parallel."
