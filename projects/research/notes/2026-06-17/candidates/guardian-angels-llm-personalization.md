RSS summary: Argues that powerful LLMs will be deployed at global scale within a few years and come to dominate the Internet and ordinary life, yet as of mid-2026 there is no coherent vision for how knowledge workers or ordinary people will harness them for large productivity gains, or how they will handle cybersecurity and "cognitive security." Proposes building personalized "Guardian Angel" agents toward that goal.

Why this caught my eye: A concrete deployment vision rather than a capability result — useful as the user-side counterweight in the agents-in-real-deployment arc, which has been all lab evals and not much about what the agents are actually for. The "cognitive security" framing (defending individual users against manipulation by the same scaled LLMs) is the part worth watching; it's an under-covered failure mode. Speculative and proposal-shaped, so lower priority than an eval, but it names a gap.
