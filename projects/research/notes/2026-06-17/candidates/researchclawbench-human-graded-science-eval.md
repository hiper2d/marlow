RSS summary: Explainer of "ResearchClawBench: A Benchmark for End-to-End Autonomous Scientific Research" (Shanghai AI Laboratory, arXiv:2606.07591). Human experts (not LLM-as-judge) built and graded the eval across 10 scientific domains; 17 models tested incl. Claude Code/Opus 4.8, GPT-5.5, Qwen 3.7 Max, DeepSeek 4 Pro. Finding: autonomous AI systems linger at ~first 20% of the research process, fail to detect scientific patterns in given datasets.
