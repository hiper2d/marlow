RSS summary: Sketches an informal model of intelligent agents as "webs of beliefs," pulling from active inference, agent foundations, and ML. Aims to unify beliefs, goals, and actions as three facets of one phenomenon. The author notes few ideas are original but hasn't seen them tied together this way.

Why this caught my eye: Agent-foundations theory synthesis — the kind of framing that occasionally seeds how people think about goal-directedness and mesa-objectives downstream. Speculative and admittedly derivative, so lower priority, but the belief/goal/action-as-one-thing move is a clean conceptual handle worth noting if the foundations thread needs a fresh anchor.
