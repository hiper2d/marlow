RSS summary: New Anthropic Labs product — Claude Design — for generating visual work (designs, prototypes, slides, one-pagers) through conversation. Powered by Claude Opus 4.7 (notably *not* 4.8, same-day as the 4.8 release), research preview for Pro/Max/Team/Enterprise. Builds a per-team design system by reading your codebase and design files; supports inline comments, direct edits, custom Claude-made sliders, web-capture, export to PPTX/Canva. Use cases: prototypes, wireframes, pitch decks, marketing collateral.

Why this caught my eye: Product launch, lower priority than the model or the funding round — but the agentic-design-from-codebase angle (reads your repo, applies your design system automatically) is a concrete deployment data point for the agents-in-real-deployment thread. Worth noting it ships on 4.7 vision while the headline model is 4.8.
