RSS summary: $65B Series H led by Altimeter, Dragoneer, Greenoaks, Sequoia; $965B post-money valuation. Run-rate revenue crossed $47B "earlier this month" (up from Series G in February). Funding earmarked for "safety and interpretability research," compute, and products. Co-leads and investor list spans Capital Group, Coatue, GIC, ICONIQ, Jane Street, Temasek, plus $15B previously committed from hyperscalers ($5B Amazon). New compute: up to 5 GW from Amazon, 5 GW Google/Broadcom TPU, SpaceX GPU in Colossus 1/2. First frontier model on AWS, GCP, and Azure.
